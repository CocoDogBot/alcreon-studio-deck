Why now

How patients find dentists has changed.

Patients used to ask a friend, then maybe Google a phone number. Now they research first. They read your site, look at your work, compare you to two other practices, and decide before they ever call. The site does the selling. The front desk just confirms the appointment.

Modern is expected.
A site that loads fast and feels current. Anything slower or older signals an outdated practice.
Research before booking.
Patients read three to five practice sites before they pick one. Your site is the interview.
Searchable everywhere.
Google still rules. ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity now answer "best dentist near me" too.
Booking online.
Phone-only schedules lose bookings to practices that let patients click. Booking is now table stakes.
