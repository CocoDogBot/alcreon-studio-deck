Why now

How patients find dentists has changed.

Patients used to ask a friend, then Google a phone number. Now they research, compare, and decide on their own before ever reaching out. Some still call, but many just book online and never pick up the phone. Your site is doing the selling, the convincing, and the closing.

Modern is expected.
A site that loads fast and feels current. Anything slower or older signals an outdated practice.
Research before booking.
Patients read three to five practice sites before they pick one. Your site is the interview.
Searchable everywhere.

Booking online.
Patients want to book without calling. If your site can't, they're booking with someone else.
